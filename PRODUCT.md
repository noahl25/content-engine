# PRODUCT — faceless explainer channel

**What:** An autonomous faceless Instagram Reels channel that publishes one animated
science + tech/AI explainer per day. Each ~45–55s vertical reel takes one concept (how DNS works,
what a CPU cache is, Docker/Kubernetes…) and explains it with animated diagrams + narration.

**Audience:** curious developers, students, and tech-adjacent people who want to "feel smart fast"
— the kind who save and share explainer reels.

**Register:** brand. This is content where the design IS the product; it should look premium and
editorial, never generic or AI-generated. Optimize for retention (strong hook), saves, and shares.

**Surface:** programmatic video built in Remotion (React), rendered vertical 1080×1920. Not a web
app — the "UI" is the video frame. Design context for it lives in `DESIGN.md` and
`memory/design-system.md`.
